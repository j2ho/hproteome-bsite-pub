Submitting Your Search

This page shows how to search for binding information for protein targets of your interest in HProteome-Bsite

You can submit search for protein targets by

  • UniProt ID (e.g. Q9H0K1)

  • Protein Name (e.g. SIK2)

  • Protein Family (e.g. GPCR)

A list of UniProt IDs matching your search will come up as result

Once you click on the UniProt ID, a list of domains will be shown

 

For a target without HProteome-BSite predictions, a reason for not having predictions is explained, and the 3D structure of the target will be shown
(e.g. UniProt ID P37840)
